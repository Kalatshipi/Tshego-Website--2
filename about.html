About Tshego

CEO Of AEON Intvestments Management, Public Speaker,Bloger and Wife.

The Beginning

Tshego was born and resid in Bloemfontein ,Free State South Africa.Tshego studied accounting at University of Free State. Garduated in year 2014,then she to Cape Town(The Moter City) in 2017.Tshego is a driven and visionary professional who is an accomplished Model United Nations Debater. She is also the former Vice-President of Students In Free Enterprise (SIFE) at the University of the Free State, now known as Enactus. She won the Free State Enterprise Job Creation Challenge for Best New Enterprise for a company she co-founded while studying. She is passionate about entrepreneurship, building great organisations and leadershi Tshego holds a Bachelor of Accounting degree from the University of the Free State. She also holds a Postgraduate Diploma in Investment Planning and a Postgraduate Diploma in Financial Planning from the same institution. She has seven years experience which includes time at PPS Investments and Sanlam Personal Finance.
